In 281 AC, a grand tourney was held at Harrenhal. Nobles from all over the Seven Kingdoms came to participate, including King Aerys II Targaryen and the entire Stark family. Prince Rhaegar Targaryen won the final tilt, but instead of crowning his wife queen of love and beauty, he instead crowned Lyanna Stark, who was betrothed to Lord Robert Baratheon. At the coming of the next year, Lyanna and Rhaegar eloped together to Dorne.

Lyanna's elder brother Brandon rode immediately to King's Landing alongside his many companions. Unaware Rhaegar was no longer in the Crownlands, he rode around the castle and challenged Rhaegar to "come out and die". The King had Brandon and all his men arrested for threatening the crown prince. Brandon's father, Lord Rickard Stark, went to King's Landing with two hundred men to negotiate for his son's release. None of them returned.

Lord Rickard demanded a trial by battle, and King Aerys agreed; however, The Mad King named fire the champion of the Targaryens and burned Lord Rickard alive. He forced Brandon to strangle himself in an attempt to save his father.

King Aeyrs then demanded that Lord Jon Arryn send the heads of Lord Robert Baratheon and now-Lord Eddard Stark to the capital. Arryn refused, and raised his banners in revolt, beginning the war in earnest.
